004 : Sense for plants

Sense for plants, an application for controlling plants with IoT sensors. The user can add plants from a database and connect them to a sensor. The sensor measures the humidity and displays the data in the overview. It is also possible to add new plants in the overview, adjust the watering times, or water the plants directly.

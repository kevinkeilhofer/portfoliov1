005 : Aldoo

A big problem for small and medium enterprises is warehousing and logistics. Transportation costs cause 2/3 of the expenses. So the challenge was to develop an efficient and at the same time cost-effective solution with high reliability. We developed our solution together with the logisticians of a local bakery. The final product is a mobile application that is networked with the transport baskets and supports the driver.
